Given English and Math test scores of students as integer arrays, find the number of students who have composite score below average.



Input Format

The first line specify the number of following input lines. Each input after the first line is list of comma separated scores of English and Math respectively




Sample Input 1:

2
200,250,700,700
400,400,400,400
Output:

2



Explanation

Given 200,250,700,700 and 400,400,400,400, your solution should return 2. The composite scores of the 4 children are 600, 650, 1100, and 1100 and the average composite score is 862.5, so 2 of the children are below average.
